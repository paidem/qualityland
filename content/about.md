Quality Land is a community-driven software testing conference organized by the non-profit association Lithuanian Software Testing Qualifications Board (LTSTQB).

Quality doesn't come from certainty. It comes from curiosity. We question what seems obvious. We test what others trust.

That's why Quality Land exists: to be heard. Not a whisper. Not just another voice - but a clear, strong signal cutting through the noise, shaped by real practice, industry standards, and the people behind them.

Built for those who dare to ask uncomfortable questions - before users do. High-quality content. Accessible price. A community that grows stronger with every shared insight.
